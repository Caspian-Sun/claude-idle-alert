## Overview

Claude Code **away-from-keyboard alert plugin** (dead-man's switch). When Claude needs your decision (asking a question, awaiting plan approval, or permission prompt), it immediately notifies you via **Feishu or DingTalk**. If you don't respond within 2/10 minutes, it escalates the reminder. Optionally supports Feishu phone urgency for critical notifications.

## Problem Solved

Claude Code users often step away from their computer while Claude waits for a critical decision. Without notification, they don't know Claude is blocked. This plugin provides a tiered alert system:
- **Instant**: Immediate webhook notification
- **Tier 1**: Reminder after 2 minutes of inactivity
- **Tier 2**: Escalation with @mention after 10 minutes
- **Tier 3** (optional): Feishu phone call after 20 minutes

## Target Users

- Claude Code users who need to be notified of blocking decisions
- Teams using Feishu/DingTalk for communication
- Users working away from keyboard during long Claude runs
